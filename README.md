# emart-digital-transformation-case-study
ISB executive case study on EMart’s digital transformation journey, omnichannel retail, AI-driven customer experience, agile transformation, and enterprise digital modernization.

### Author
Manisha Kriplani



# Overview

This repository contains my ISB executive case study focused on the digital transformation journey of EMart, a traditional retail business evolving into an AI-driven omnichannel digital ecosystem.

The project explores how retail enterprises can modernize through:
- Digital transformation
- AI-driven customer engagement
- Cloud-native technologies
- Omnichannel experiences
- Agile operating models
- Data-driven decision-making

The study highlights the transformation of EMart from a traditional retail store into a scalable, intelligent, customer-centric digital platform.



# Business Problem

Traditional retail businesses face several challenges including:
- Legacy systems
- Fragmented customer data
- High operational costs
- Resistance to digital adoption
- Limited personalization
- Supply chain inefficiencies

To remain competitive in the digital economy, EMart adopted a comprehensive digital-first transformation strategy.
